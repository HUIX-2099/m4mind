Privacy Policy

M4M Studios – Media For the Mind | Effective Date: March 2026

1. Introduction

M4M Studios ("M4M," "we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our services, or interact with us.

2. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Business or organization name
  • Payment and billing information

b. Usage Data:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on site
  • Device information

c. Media & Content:

  • Audio, video, and photographic content
  • Project files and media assets

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and manage studio services
  • Process bookings and payments
  • Communicate with you regarding services and updates
  • Improve our website and user experience
  • Deliver marketing or promotional materials (if opted in)
  • Maintain security and prevent misuse

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers (payment processors, hosting platforms)
  • Legal authorities if required by law
  • Business partners (only with your consent)

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure servers

  • Restricted access to sensitive data

However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent
  • Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at: info@m4tmind.com

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to:

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.
