FAQ

Common Questions

How quickly can you start on a project?
Most projects can begin within 1–2 weeks of signing. For urgent requests, we often accommodate faster turnarounds — just let us know your timeline in the form above.
Do you work outside of Liberia?
Yes! While we're based in Monrovia, we serve clients across Africa and globally. We provide remote podcast production, virtual event management, and can travel for documentary projects.
What does a typical project cost?
Pricing varies by project scope, duration, and service type. After our initial consultation we'll send you a detailed, transparent quote. We work with organizations of all sizes and can tailor packages to your budget.
How do I prepare for our first meeting?
Just come with your idea! It helps to have a rough sense of your timeline, target audience, and goals — but we'll guide you through the rest. No technical knowledge required.
Do you offer payment plans?
Yes, we're flexible. Typically we require a 50% deposit to start, with the balance due on delivery. For larger projects, milestone-based payment plans are available.
